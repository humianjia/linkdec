How do I report a game that does not load?

Send the game page URL, the browser version, the device type, and the exact issue you saw. A screenshot or short screen recording is helpful when available.

Where can I review your policies first?

Start with the Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, Terms of Service, and Content Policy. Those pages explain the current public handling of data, cookies, site usage, and content requests.
