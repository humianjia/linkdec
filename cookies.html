Cookie Policy

How cookies and local storage may be used on the public site.

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. Similar technologies such as local storage may also be used to remember settings or support measurement and advertising.

2. Why We Use Them

  • To remember basic site preferences
  • To understand whether pages load correctly and how they are used
  • To support ad delivery and ad measurement on pages where ads are enabled

3. Types Of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: help core pages and embeds function properly
  • Measurement cookies: help us understand aggregate visits and performance
  • Advertising cookies: may be used by advertising partners such as Google to deliver and measure relevant ads

4. Third-Party Services

When advertising or third-party embedded game content is present, those providers may set or read their own cookies according to their own policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can block or remove cookies through your browser settings. Doing so may affect how some embedded content or saved preferences behave on the site.

6. Related Policies

This page works alongside the Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Content Policy. For privacy questions, contact privacy@trafficgames.online.
