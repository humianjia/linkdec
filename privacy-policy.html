Privacy Policy

How the public site handles basic usage, advertising, and contact data.

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to the public website available at trafficgames.online. It covers editorial pages, support pages, and featured playable pages that are published on the site.

2. Information We May Collect

When you browse the site, standard server and browser information may be processed automatically. This can include:

  • IP address and approximate location information
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Referring pages, visited URLs, and timestamps
  • Basic diagnostic data related to page loading and embedded game playback

3. Why We Use This Information

We use limited usage and diagnostic information to keep the website available, understand page performance, reduce broken experiences, and support advertising and measurement where enabled.

4. Cookies And Similar Technologies

The site may use cookies or similar storage for essential preferences, traffic measurement, and ad delivery. More detail is provided in the Cookie Policy.

5. Advertising

Some public pages use Google AdSense or similar advertising technologies. These services may use cookies or device identifiers to deliver and measure ads. You can review Google's ads information at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Third-Party Content

Certain playable experiences may be embedded from third-party game providers. When you interact with an embedded game, the provider may process data according to its own policies and technical requirements. We recommend reviewing any applicable third-party notices where available.

7. Data Retention

We aim to keep site-level support and diagnostic information only for as long as reasonably necessary for maintenance, compliance, and abuse prevention.

8. Your Choices

You can manage cookies through browser settings, restrict certain advertising preferences through provider tools, and contact us if you need help locating the right policy or contact channel.

9. Children's Privacy

The site is intended for a general audience and is not knowingly directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through a public contact channel, please email us so we can review the request.

10. Updates To This Policy

We may update this page as the public site structure, advertising setup, or support processes change. The latest revision date appears at the top of the page.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email privacy@trafficgames.online. General site issues can be sent to support@trafficgames.online.

12. Related Content Standards

Questions about page ownership, editorial summaries, or content removal can be reviewed through the public Content Policy page.
